Mini Cascadia (Solo Hex)

Open-ended hex board, tile-then-token turns, and end-game largest-group scoring.

Mode: Full (20 turns)

Turn: 1 / 20

Phase: Pick a pair

Pair Market

Select one pair. Place the tile first, then place its token on any legal tile.

Open-Ended Hex Board

Rules in this Version

  • Starter setup: a pre-placed 3-hex triangle is on the board.
  • Tiles can only be placed on open hexes adjacent to existing tiles.
  • Each tile has two printed animals; only those tokens may be placed there.
  • If your picked token has no legal placement anywhere, it is discarded.
  • After every turn, one random remaining market pair is discarded, then market refills to 4.
  • Scoring is only at game end: for each terrain and each animal, score the size of your single largest connected group.
